Make Smart Packing Lists

Use PackMap to create smart and personalized packing lists for your vacations and trips. Enter in the info for your trip including the destination, dates, and activities you plan on doing and our app will generate a smart packing list of what you should bring. We use the destination of your trip in order to gather weather data of your destination to help know what clothing you should bring. You can then edit and add to the packing list to make sure you are fully prepared for your trip.

Plan Your Activities and Outfits

Use the Trip Overview feature to see each day of your trip where you can look at the weather, your planned activities for that day, and you can upload images of your outfits. The outfit feature is useful to plan out when you will wear what you are packing, so that you wear the right outfit for each day based off of your activities and the weather. This way you can enjoy your vacation without waking up each day worrying about what to wear, what's clean, and what clothes would work with your hike or fancy dinner.

Stay Organized During Your Vacation

With PackMap you can look at your packing list and trip overview throughout your trip to make sure you always repack everything back in your suitcase and to stay on track with your schedule of planned activities and outfit management. This way, when you are leaving your travel destination you won't end up accidentally forgetting your charger at the hotel!
